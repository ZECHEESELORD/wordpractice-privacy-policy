Rules

Automation

Usage of self-bots, scripts, macros or anything allowing automation of commands is against the rules.

Spamming of commands is also not allowed.

Exploiting

Sharing or using exploits/bugs is not allowed, you can report these through the support server.

Advertisement

Usage of the bot to advertise is forbidden, including advertising through your username on leaderboards.

Exchanging

Exchanging another form of currency for wordPractice coins is forbidden.

Account-Sharing

Sharing an account is considered cheating because it provides an unfair advantage.

Cheating

Using any form of OCR or cheating software is against the rules. Distribution of these software to other users is also forbidden.

Note:

Staff can punish users based on their best judgment. If you feel that a staff member has been unjust in their decision, reach out to another moderator or owner.

Banned users can submit a ban appeal by contacting one of the moderators through the support server.

Rules

Automation:

Usage of self-bots, scripts, macros or anything allowing automation of commands or typing is against the rules.

Spamming of commands is also not allowed. We define spamming as running eight or move commands in ten seconds.

Exploiting:

Sharing or using exploits/bugs is strictly forbidden. You must report these through the support server by creating a ticket.

Advertisement

Usage of the bot to advertise any service not affiliated with wordPractice, including advertising through your username on leaderboards is bannable. Moderation actions will be taken and affected users will not be reimbursed.

Boosting

1. Exchanging another form of currency (Real life dollars, or other currencies) for wordPractice coins is forbidden.

2. Sharing accounts with the purpose of gaining an unfair advantage is also considered boosting.

Cheating

Using any form of OCR or cheating software to gain an unfair advantage is against the rules. Distribution of these software to other users is also forbidden.

Note:

Staff perform moderation actions based on their best judgment. If you feel that a staff member has been unjust in their decision, reach out to the moderation team via ticket.

Banned users can submit a ban appeal by contacting one of the moderators through the support server.
